Frequently Asked Questions ❓

1. How do I place an order?

You can place an order directly through our website by browsing our Products section, selecting the cake or item you like, and adding it to your cart. Once you’re ready, proceed to checkout and complete the payment process. You’ll receive an order confirmation email once your order has been placed.

2. Can I customize my cake?

Yes, we offer custom cake options! You can choose different flavors, fillings, designs, and decorations. For custom orders, please visit our store/site for Special Orders page or contact us directly at customcakes@cakedelivery.com with your request.

3. What should I do if my cake arrives damaged?

If your cake arrives damaged, please contact us immediately at (123) 456-7890 or email support@cakedelivery.com with photos of the damage. We will do our best to resolve the issue, either by offering a replacement or providing a refund, depending on the situation.

4. Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options?

Yes, we offer a range of gluten-free and vegan cakes. You can filter these options in our Products section or contact us to discuss any special dietary requirements.

5. How should I store my cake once it is delivered?

Most of our cakes should be kept refrigerated if you’re not serving them right away. However, we recommend taking the cake out of the refrigerator about 30 minutes before serving to let it come to room temperature for the best taste.
